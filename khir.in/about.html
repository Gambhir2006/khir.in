Our Mission

At KHIR.IN, our mission is to bridge the gap between job seekers and employers across India. We strive to provide the most accurate, up-to-date, and comprehensive job information to help millions of Indians find their dream careers.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality job opportunities, and we're committed to making the job search process transparent, efficient, and successful for all.

Our Story

2024

The Beginning

KHIR.IN was founded with a simple goal: to make job searching easier for millions of Indians. Started as a small blog with just 100 daily visitors.

2025

Rapid Growth

Expanded to cover all major job categories. Reached 100,000 monthly users and became a trusted source for government job updates.

2026

Market Leader

Now serving over 500,000 job seekers monthly. Partnered with major companies and government agencies for authentic job listings.

Our Core Values

Accuracy

We verify every job listing to ensure 100% accuracy and authenticity. No fake jobs, no false promises.

Timeliness

Job updates are published within hours of official announcements. Never miss an opportunity due to delayed information.

User-First

Our platform is designed keeping job seekers in mind. Free access, easy navigation, and comprehensive information.

Trust

Built on trust and transparency. We never charge for job information and never share user data without consent.
